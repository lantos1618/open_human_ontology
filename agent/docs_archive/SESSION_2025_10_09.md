### 2. Blood Cell & Hematology System (`src/systems/cardiovascular/`)

#### New Module: `blood_cells.rs`
Comprehensive cellular models for all blood cell types:

**Red Blood Cells (RBC)**
- Morphology types (Normocytic, Microcytic, Macrocytic, Sickle, etc.)
- Oxygen capacity calculation
- Senescence detection (120-day lifespan)
- Deformability tracking

**Neutrophils**
- Activation states (Resting → Primed → Activated → Exhausted)
- Granule content (azurophilic, specific, gelatinase, secretory vesicles)
- Phagocytic capacity and oxidative burst
- Location tracking (circulation, marginated, tissue, bone marrow)
- 8-hour lifespan modeling

**Lymphocytes**
- T cells (CD4 Helper, CD8 Cytotoxic, Regulatory, Gamma-Delta)
- B cells (Naive, Memory, Plasma, Marginal)
- NK cells and NKT cells
- Memory status (Naive, Central, Effector, Resident)
- Differentiation pathways (B cell → Plasma cell)
- Surface marker and cytokine production tracking

**Monocytes & Macrophages**
- Monocyte subsets (Classical, Intermediate, Non-classical)
- Macrophage differentiation pathway
- Polarization states (M0, M1, M2a/b/c)
- Tissue-specific macrophages (lung, liver, spleen, brain, etc.)

**Eosinophils**
- Granule proteins (MBP, EPO, ECP, EDN)
- Activation and degranulation states

**Basophils**
- Histamine and heparin content
- Activation tracking

**Platelets**
- Activation cascade (Resting → Shape Change → Activated → Aggregated)
- Granule types (alpha, dense, lysosomal)
- Surface receptor mapping
- 10-day lifespan with senescence detection

#### New Module: `bone_marrow.rs`
Complete hematopoietic system modeling:

**Marrow Locations & Types**
- Anatomical locations (skull, vertebrae, ribs, sternum, pelvis, femur, humerus)
- Marrow types (Red, Yellow, Gelatinous)
- Cellularity percentage by location

**Stem Cell Compartment**
- Hematopoietic stem cells (HSC)
- Multipotent progenitors
- Self-renewal rate
- Mobilization capacity

**Erythroid Lineage**
- Complete maturation stages: Proerythroblast → Basophilic → Polychromatic → Orthochromatic → Reticulocyte
- EPO (erythropoietin) stimulation response
- Production rate: 2×10¹¹ cells/day

**Myeloid Lineage**
- Neutrophil development: Myeloblast → Promyelocyte → Myelocyte → Metamyelocyte → Band → Segmented
- Eosinophil and basophil precursors
- Monocyte precursors
- G-CSF sensitivity

**Lymphoid Lineage**
- B cell development (pro-B, pre-B, immature B)
- T cell precursors (pro-T, pre-T)

**Megakaryocyte Lineage**
- Ploidy distribution (2N to 64N)
- Average ploidy calculation
- Platelet production: 1×10¹¹ cells/day
- TPO (thrombopoietin) sensitivity

**Stromal Microenvironment**
- Support cells: fibroblasts, endothelial cells, adipocytes, osteoblasts, macrophages
- Cytokine network (SCF, IL-3, IL-6, GM-CSF)
- Extracellular matrix (collagen, fibronectin, laminin, proteoglycans)

**Marrow Analytics**
- Myeloid:Erythroid (M:E) ratio calculation
- Total cell production per day
- Activity status determination
