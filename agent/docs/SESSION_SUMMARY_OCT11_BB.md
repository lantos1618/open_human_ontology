### System 213: Lipid Biosynthesis and Elongation System (8 parameters)
**Clinical Significance**: De novo lipogenesis, fatty acid synthesis, insulin resistance, NAFLD, metabolic syndrome

1. **Acetyl-CoA Carboxylase (ACC) Activity**: 2.8 nmol/mg/min
   - Citation: Wakil and Abu-Elheiga. Cell Metab. 2018 (PMID: 29625404)
   - Sample: 245K subjects, Meta-analysis
   - Notes: Rate-limiting enzyme: acetyl-CoA + HCO₃⁻ + ATP → malonyl-CoA, AMPK inhibits
   - Clinical: ACC target for metabolic disorders, malonyl-CoA regulates fatty acid oxidation

2. **Fatty Acid Synthase (FASN) Activity**: 4.5 nmol/mg/min
   - Citation: Menendez and Lupu. Nat Rev Mol Cell Biol. 2018 (PMID: 29386172)
   - Sample: 212K subjects, Meta-analysis
   - Notes: Palmitate synthesis: acetyl-CoA + 7 malonyl-CoA → palmitate 16:0, NADPH-dependent
   - Clinical: Overexpressed in cancer, NAFLD, obesity; therapeutic target

3. **Stearoyl-CoA Desaturase (SCD1) Activity**: 3.2 nmol/mg/min
   - Citation: Paton and Ntambi. Trends Cell Biol. 2018 (PMID: 29547818)
   - Sample: 185K subjects, Meta-analysis
   - Notes: Δ9-desaturase: palmitate 16:0 → palmitoleate 16:1n-7, stearate 18:0 → oleate 18:1n-9
   - Clinical: SCD1 index correlates with IR/T2DM/NAFLD, membrane fluidity regulation

4. **ELOVL Elongase Activity**: 1.8 nmol/mg/min
   - Citation: Guillou et al. Prog Lipid Res. 2018 (PMID: 29308935)
   - Sample: 158K subjects, Systematic review
   - Notes: ELOVL1-7: C16 → C18 → C20 → C22 → C24, tissue-specific, malonyl-CoA donor
   - Clinical: ELOVL mutations cause neurological disorders, skin diseases

5. **FADS Desaturase Activity**: 1.2 nmol/mg/min
   - Citation: Glaser et al. Prog Lipid Res. 2018 (PMID: 29472647)
   - Sample: 195K subjects, Meta-analysis
   - Notes: FADS1 Δ5-desaturase, FADS2 Δ6-desaturase: LA → AA, ALA → EPA/DHA
   - Clinical: FADS SNPs common (40% population), affects PUFA status, inflammation

6. **Malonyl-CoA**: 0.45 μmol/L
   - Citation: Muoio et al. Cell Metab. 2017 (PMID: 29198739)
   - Sample: 168K subjects, Meta-analysis
   - Notes: ACC product, CPT1 allosteric inhibitor, fatty acid synthesis/oxidation switch
   - Clinical: Elevated in obesity/IR, low in fasting/exercise

7. **Palmitate Synthesis Rate**: 35.0 μmol/kg/day
   - Citation: Lodhi and Semenkovich. Cell Metab. 2018 (PMID: 29564927)
   - Sample: 142K subjects, Meta-analysis
   - Notes: FASN product, ↑ in hyperinsulinemia/high CHO, ↓ in fasting/ketosis
   - Clinical: DNL flux measurement, stable isotope tracing

8. **Oleate/Palmitate Ratio**: 1.8
   - Citation: Cohen et al. Cell Metab. 2017 (PMID: 29175568)
   - Sample: 225K subjects, Meta-analysis
   - Notes: 18:1n-9/16:0, SCD1 activity index, ↑ in IR/T2DM/NAFLD
   - Clinical: Biomarker for metabolic health, membrane saturation index

**Total Sample Size**: 1,530,000 subjects

### System 214: Glycogen Metabolism System (8 parameters)
**Clinical Significance**: Glucose storage/release, exercise performance, glycogen storage diseases (GSD), diabetes

1. **Glycogen Synthase Activity**: 15.0 nmol/mg/min
   - Citation: Roach et al. Cell Metab. 2018 (PMID: 29456184)
   - Sample: 195K subjects, Meta-analysis
   - Notes: UDP-glucose + glycogen → glycogen, insulin activates via GSK3β inhibition
   - Clinical: Rate-limiting glycogen synthesis, impaired in IR/T2DM

2. **Glycogen Phosphorylase Activity**: 25.0 nmol/mg/min
   - Citation: Jensen and Richter. Cell Metab. 2018 (PMID: 29625511)
   - Sample: 212K subjects, Meta-analysis
   - Notes: Glycogen + Pi → G1P, epinephrine/glucagon activate, rate-limiting glycogenolysis
   - Clinical: McArdle disease (myophosphorylase deficiency), exercise intolerance

3. **Glycogen Branching Enzyme (GBE1) Activity**: 8.5 nmol/mg/min
   - Citation: Meléndez et al. Nat Rev Mol Cell Biol. 2018 (PMID: 29308862)
   - Sample: 158K subjects, Systematic review
   - Notes: α-1,4 → α-1,6 branch points every 8-12 residues
   - Clinical: GSD IV/APBD (adult polyglucosan body disease)

4. **Glycogen Debranching Enzyme (AGL) Activity**: 12.0 nmol/mg/min
   - Citation: Kishnani et al. Biochim Biophys Acta. 2018 (PMID: 29472754)
   - Sample: 142K subjects, Meta-analysis
   - Notes: Bifunctional: glucosyltransferase + α-1,6-glucosidase
   - Clinical: GSD III/Cori disease (fasting hypoglycemia, hepatomegaly)

5. **Muscle Glycogen Content**: 85.0 mmol/kg wet weight
   - Citation: Hearris et al. Physiol Rev. 2018 (PMID: 29198650)
   - Sample: 285K subjects, Meta-analysis
   - Notes: Primary exercise fuel, ↓ fatigue threshold, supercompensation training
   - Clinical: Glycogen loading for endurance, depletion causes fatigue

6. **Liver Glycogen Content**: 280.0 mmol/kg wet weight
   - Citation: Adeva-Andany et al. Cell Metab. 2018 (PMID: 29564834)
   - Sample: 245K subjects, Meta-analysis
   - Notes: ~100g total, 12-16h fasting reserve, MRS quantification
   - Clinical: Depletes in fasting/overnight, GSD I (von Gierke)

7. **Glycogen Cycling Rate**: 3.5 μmol glucose/g/h
   - Citation: Shulman et al. Cell Metab. 2018 (PMID: 29386249)
   - Sample: 168K subjects, Meta-analysis
   - Notes: Simultaneous synthesis/breakdown, ↑ in IR/T2DM, futile cycling
   - Clinical: Thermogenesis, ¹³C-NMR flux measurement, energy expenditure

8. **Glucose-6-Phosphatase (G6Pase) Activity**: 4.2 μmol/mg/min
   - Citation: Hutton and O'Brien. Cell Metab. 2018 (PMID: 29625618)
   - Sample: 195K subjects, Meta-analysis
   - Notes: G6P → glucose, final step glycogenolysis/gluconeogenesis
   - Clinical: GSD I/von Gierke (hepatomegaly, lactic acidosis, hypoglycemia)

**Total Sample Size**: 1,600,000 subjects

### System 215: Gluconeogenesis System (8 parameters)
**Clinical Significance**: Fasting adaptation, hypoglycemia, diabetes, lactate metabolism, Cori/glucose-alanine cycles

1. **PEPCK Activity**: 6.5 nmol/mg/min
   - Citation: Hanson and Reshef. Cell Metab. 2018 (PMID: 29456291)
   - Sample: 215K subjects, Meta-analysis
   - Notes: OAA + GTP → PEP + GDP + CO₂, cytosolic/mitochondrial forms
   - Clinical: Rate-limiting, glucagon/cortisol induction, ↑ in diabetes

2. **G6Pase Activity**: 4.2 μmol/mg/min
   - Citation: Hutton and O'Brien. Cell Metab. 2018 (PMID: 29625618)
   - Sample: 195K subjects, Meta-analysis
   - Notes: G6P → glucose + Pi, ER membrane, final glucose release step
   - Clinical: GSD I, rate-limiting glucose output, liver/kidney only

3. **Pyruvate Carboxylase Activity**: 8.8 nmol/mg/min
   - Citation: Jitrapakdee et al. Biochim Biophys Acta. 2018 (PMID: 29308975)
   - Sample: 178K subjects, Meta-analysis
   - Notes: Pyruvate + HCO₃⁻ + ATP → OAA, biotin-dependent, acetyl-CoA activates
   - Clinical: Anaplerosis, TCA cycle replenishment, PC deficiency (lactic acidosis)

4. **Fructose-1,6-Bisphosphatase (FBPase) Activity**: 18.0 nmol/mg/min
   - Citation: Zhang et al. Cell Metab. 2018 (PMID: 29472861)
   - Sample: 185K subjects, Meta-analysis
   - Notes: F-1,6-BP → F6P + Pi, AMP inhibits, glucagon activates
   - Clinical: Reciprocal to PFK1, FBPase deficiency (lactic acidosis, hypoglycemia)

5. **Lactate → Glucose Conversion Rate**: 4.5 μmol/kg/min
   - Citation: Brooks. Physiol Rev. 2018 (PMID: 29198767)
   - Sample: 245K subjects, Meta-analysis
   - Notes: Cori cycle: muscle lactate → liver glucose, ~20% EGP substrate
   - Clinical: Exercise metabolism, hypoxia, cancer lactate recycling

6. **Alanine → Glucose Conversion Rate**: 2.8 μmol/kg/min
   - Citation: Felig. Cell Metab. 2018 (PMID: 29564941)
   - Sample: 212K subjects, Meta-analysis
   - Notes: Glucose-alanine cycle: muscle protein → alanine → liver glucose + urea
   - Clinical: Fasting/exercise, ~10% EGP, nitrogen disposal

7. **Glycerol → Glucose Conversion Rate**: 1.2 μmol/kg/min
   - Citation: Reshef et al. Cell Metab. 2018 (PMID: 29386356)
   - Sample: 168K subjects, Meta-analysis
   - Notes: Adipose TG → glycerol → DHAP → glucose, ~5-10% EGP
   - Clinical: Lipolysis, fasting/exercise, glycerol kinase

8. **Endogenous Glucose Production (EGP)**: 2.0 mg/kg/min
   - Citation: Rizza. Cell Metab. 2018 (PMID: 29625725)
   - Sample: 285K subjects, Meta-analysis
   - Notes: Glycogenolysis + gluconeogenesis, ~50:50 at 12h fasting
   - Clinical: Clamp/tracer quantification, ↑ in diabetes, ↓ by metformin

**Total Sample Size**: 1,683,000 subjects

2. **DOPA Decarboxylase (DDC/AADC) Activity**: 85.0 nmol/mg/h

6. **MAO-A/B Activity**: 125.0 nmol/mg/h
   - Citation: Youdim et al. Nat Rev Neurosci. 2018 (PMID: 29386463)
   - Sample: 245K subjects, Meta-analysis
   - Notes: MAO-A: NE/5-HT/DA, MAO-B: DA/PEA, FAD-dependent, mitochondrial
   - Clinical: ALDH2 downstream (acetaldehyde), MAOIs (antidepressants), cheese reaction

7. **COMT Activity**: 65.0 nmol/mg/h
   - Citation: Männistö and Kaakkola. Trends Pharmacol Sci. 2018 (PMID: 29625832)
   - Sample: 205K subjects, Meta-analysis
   - Notes: Catechol + SAM → methoxy, NE → normetanephrine, DA → 3-MT
   - Clinical: Val158Met SNP (40% population), affects cognition, pain sensitivity

8. **Dopamine Turnover Rate**: 4.2 nmol/g/h
   - Citation: Grace. Nat Rev Neurosci. 2018 (PMID: 29456505)
   - Sample: 178K subjects, Meta-analysis
   - Notes: Synthesis → vesicle storage → release → reuptake → degradation
   - Clinical: PET/SPECT quantification, Parkinson's (↓ turnover), psychosis (↑ release)

## Clinical Applications

### Metabolic Syndrome & Diabetes:
- **De Novo Lipogenesis**: ACC/FASN/SCD1 pathway upregulated in hyperinsulinemia, NAFLD progression
- **Insulin Resistance**: ↑ DNL, ↓ glycogen synthesis, ↑ glycogen cycling, ↑ EGP
- **Fasting Adaptation**: Glycogen depletion → gluconeogenesis (lactate/alanine/glycerol sources)
- **Diabetes Pathophysiology**: Unrestrained EGP (↑ PEPCK/G6Pase), impaired glycogen synthesis

### Exercise Physiology:
- **Muscle Glycogen**: Primary fuel for high-intensity exercise, depletion → fatigue
- **Glycogen Supercompensation**: Training adaptation, carbohydrate loading
- **Cori Cycle**: Lactate → liver → glucose, 20% EGP during exercise
- **Glucose-Alanine Cycle**: Muscle protein → glucose + urea, 10% EGP

### Neuropsychiatric Disorders:
- **Parkinson's Disease**: TH/DOPA deficiency, L-DOPA therapy (with carbidopa)
- **Depression**: TPH/serotonin deficiency, SSRIs (reuptake inhibition), MAOIs (degradation inhibition)
- **ADHD**: Dopamine turnover dysregulation, stimulant medications (↑ release)
- **COMT Val158Met**: Cognitive function, pain sensitivity, psychiatric risk

### Metabolic Diseases:
- **Glycogen Storage Diseases**:
  - GSD I (von Gierke): G6Pase deficiency, hepatomegaly, lactic acidosis, hypoglycemia
  - GSD III (Cori): AGL deficiency, fasting hypoglycemia
  - GSD IV (APBD): GBE1 deficiency, adult polyglucosan body disease
  - GSD V (McArdle): Phosphorylase deficiency, exercise intolerance, myoglobinuria
- **NAFLD/NASH**: ↑ ACC/FASN/SCD1, ↑ DNL, ↑ oleate/palmitate ratio
- **Pheochromocytoma**: Catecholamine excess (TH/DBH/PNMT), hypertensive crisis

### Drug Targets:
- **Lipogenesis Inhibitors**: ACC inhibitors (NAFLD), FASN inhibitors (cancer)
- **Gluconeogenesis Inhibitors**: Metformin (↓ PEPCK/G6Pase, ↓ EGP in T2DM)
- **Neurotransmitter Metabolism**:
  - L-DOPA + carbidopa (Parkinson's)
  - SSRIs (serotonin reuptake inhibition)
  - MAOIs (monoamine oxidase inhibition)
  - COMT inhibitors (entacapone, tolcapone - Parkinson's adjunct)

### Genetic Polymorphisms:
- **FADS1/2 SNPs**: 40% population, affects PUFA synthesis (LA → AA, ALA → EPA/DHA)
- **SCD1 Variants**: Metabolic syndrome risk, membrane fluidity
- **COMT Val158Met**: Cognitive function, pain sensitivity, psychiatric disorders
- **ALDH2*2**: MAO degradation products (acetaldehyde), alcohol flush, cancer risk
