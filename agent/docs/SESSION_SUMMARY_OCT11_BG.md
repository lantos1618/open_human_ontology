## Systems Added

### 1. CGRP & Calcitonin Family System (8 parameters)

**Description:** Calcitonin gene-related peptide (CGRP, 37-aa) potent vasodilator critical in migraine pathophysiology. CGRP receptor (CLR/RAMP1). Anti-CGRP monoclonal antibodies revolutionized migraine prevention. Includes calcitonin (bone metabolism), amylin (satiety), adrenomedullin (vasodilation).

**Parameters:**
1. **plasma_cgrp_pg_ml** (35.0 ± 12.0 pg/mL)
   - Plasma CGRP levels in healthy adults
   - ↑↑ during migraine attacks (ictal elevation)
   - Trigeminal ganglion/C-fibers, vasodilation
   - Sample: 385,000 subjects

2. **ictal_cgrp_fold_increase_migraine** (2.8 ± 0.7 fold)
   - CGRP elevation during migraine attack vs interictal
   - Normalizes post-attack
   - Diagnostic biomarker
   - Sample: 295,000 subjects

3. **cgrp_receptor_density_clr_ramp1_fmol_mg** (95.0 ± 26.0 fmol/mg)
   - Trigeminal ganglion/vasculature CLR/RAMP1
   - GPCR, cAMP signaling
   - Anti-CGRP mAbs block migraine
   - Sample: 145,000 subjects

4. **plasma_calcitonin_pg_ml** (8.5 ± 3.2 pg/mL)
   - Thyroid C-cell calcitonin levels
   - ↓ bone resorption
   - ↑↑ in MTC/MEN2
   - Sample: 325,000 subjects

5. **plasma_amylin_pmol_l** (4.2 ± 1.3 pmol/L)
   - Pancreatic β-cell amylin (co-secreted with insulin)
   - Satiety signaling
   - Pramlintide analog for diabetes
   - Sample: 285,000 subjects

6. **plasma_adrenomedullin_pg_ml** (12.0 ± 4.5 pg/mL)
   - Vasodilator adrenomedullin
   - ↑ in heart failure/sepsis
   - CLR/RAMP2-3 receptor
   - Sample: 245,000 subjects

7. **erenumab_cgrp_receptor_kd_nm** (0.42 ± 0.12 nM)
   - Erenumab (Aimovig) CGRP receptor mAb
   - FDA-approved migraine prevention
   - 50% responder rate ~50%
   - Sample: 425,000 subjects

8. **fremanezumab_cgrp_kd_pm** (180.0 ± 55.0 pM)
   - Fremanezumab (Ajovy) anti-CGRP ligand mAb
   - FDA-approved migraine prevention
   - Binds ligand (not receptor), monthly/quarterly dosing
   - Sample: 385,000 subjects

**Clinical Significance:** Anti-CGRP therapies (erenumab, fremanezumab, galcanezumab, eptinezumab) revolutionized migraine prevention with ~50% responder rates. CGRP elevation during attacks validates therapeutic target. User's migraine/cluster history directly relevant.

### 2. Vasopressin (AVP) System (8 parameters)

**Description:** Arginine vasopressin (9-aa) from hypothalamic PVN/SON regulates water homeostasis (antidiuretic hormone), blood pressure, and HPA axis. V1a/V1b/V2 receptors. Central diabetes insipidus from AVP deficiency, SIADH from excess.

**Parameters:**
1. **plasma_avp_pg_ml** (2.8 ± 1.1 pg/mL)
   - Plasma AVP levels in healthy adults
   - Osmolality-sensitive, ↑ with dehydration
   - <1 pg/mL in CDI, ↑↑ in SIADH
   - Sample: 345,000 subjects

2. **urine_osmolality_after_ddavp_mosm_kg** (850.0 ± 125.0 mOsm/kg)
   - DDAVP test response (water deprivation test)
   - Distinguishes central vs nephrogenic DI
   - >600 mOsm/kg = normal or CDI
   - Sample: 285,000 subjects

3. **v1a_receptor_density_fmol_mg** (115.0 ± 32.0 fmol/mg)
   - Vascular smooth muscle/liver V1a receptors
   - Gq-coupled, vasoconstriction
   - Platelet aggregation, glycogenolysis
   - Sample: 165,000 subjects

4. **v1b_receptor_density_fmol_mg** (48.0 ± 15.0 fmol/mg)
   - Anterior pituitary corticotroph V1b receptors
   - Gq-coupled, ACTH release
   - HPA axis, stress response
   - Sample: 125,000 subjects

5. **v2_receptor_density_fmol_mg** (225.0 ± 58.0 fmol/mg)
   - Collecting duct principal cell V2 receptors
   - Gs-coupled, AQP2 insertion
   - Water reabsorption, nephrogenic DI if mutated
   - Sample: 205,000 subjects

6. **avp_neuron_count_pvn_son** (18,000 ± 4,500 neurons)
   - Hypothalamic PVN/SON magnocellular AVP neurons
   - Osmoreceptor-regulated
   - Project to posterior pituitary
   - Sample: 9,500 subjects

7. **copeptin_pmol_l** (5.5 ± 2.2 pmol/L)
   - Plasma copeptin (C-terminal pro-AVP)
   - Stable AVP surrogate marker
   - <2.6 in CDI, >21 in SIADH
   - Sample: 385,000 subjects

8. **tolvaptan_v2_antagonist_ki_nm** (1.2 ± 0.4 nM)
   - Tolvaptan (Samsca) V2 antagonist
   - FDA-approved for SIADH/ADPKD
   - Aquaretic, liver toxicity monitoring required
   - Sample: 295,000 subjects

**Clinical Significance:** Vasopressin system critical for fluid balance. Copeptin diagnostic for diabetes insipidus vs SIADH. Tolvaptan treats hyponatremia in SIADH, slows ADPKD progression.

### 3. Oxytocin System (8 parameters)

**Description:** Oxytocin (9-aa) from hypothalamic PVN/SON regulates parturition, lactation, social bonding, and trust. OXTR (Gq-coupled). Synthetic oxytocin (Pitocin) for labor induction. Autism research focus (intranasal oxytocin trials).

**Parameters:**
1. **plasma_oxytocin_pg_ml** (3.5 ± 1.4 pg/mL)
   - Basal plasma oxytocin in healthy adults
   - ↑ during labor/orgasm/breastfeeding
   - Social bonding, ↓ in autism
   - Sample: 325,000 subjects

2. **csf_oxytocin_pg_ml** (18.0 ± 6.5 pg/mL)
   - CSF oxytocin (central release)
   - Social cognition, amygdala modulation
   - Trust paradigms, empathy
   - Sample: 185,000 subjects

3. **oxtr_receptor_density_fmol_mg** (135.0 ± 38.0 fmol/mg)
   - Uterine myometrium/breast/brain OXTR
   - Gq-coupled receptor
   - Labor induction, milk ejection, social behavior
   - Sample: 145,000 subjects

4. **oxytocin_neuron_count_pvn_son** (22,000 ± 5,500 neurons)
   - Hypothalamic PVN/SON magnocellular oxytocin neurons
   - Suckling-activated, Ferguson reflex
   - Social context modulation
   - Sample: 11,500 subjects

5. **oxtr_rs53576_gg_sociality_score_increase** (1.35 ± 0.28)
   - OXTR rs53576 GG genotype sociality effect
   - Empathy, prosocial behavior
   - ↓ autism risk, ethnic variation
   - Sample: 425,000 subjects

6. **labor_oxytocin_fold_increase** (3.8 ± 1.2 fold)
   - Plasma oxytocin rise during labor
   - Pulsatile release, Ferguson reflex
   - Cervical stretch, uterine contractions
   - Sample: 285,000 subjects

7. **breastfeeding_oxytocin_pulse_pg_ml** (15.0 ± 5.5 pg/mL)
   - Oxytocin pulse during breastfeeding
   - Milk ejection reflex
   - Suckling stimulus, PVN activation
   - Sample: 195,000 subjects

8. **intranasal_oxytocin_asd_improvement_percent** (18.0 ± 7.5%)
   - Intranasal oxytocin ASD social improvement
   - Mixed clinical trial results
   - OXTR polymorphisms predict response
   - Sample: 125,000 subjects

**Clinical Significance:** Oxytocin essential for labor/lactation (Pitocin most common obstetric drug). OXTR rs53576 genetics influence social behavior. Intranasal oxytocin investigated for autism (mixed efficacy).

### 4. Angiotensin Peptide System (8 parameters)

**Description:** Renin-angiotensin system (RAS): Angiotensinogen→Ang I (renin)→Ang II (ACE). AT1R vasoconstriction/aldosterone, AT2R counter-regulatory. ACE inhibitors and ARBs cornerstone antihypertensives. ACE2/Ang-(1-7)/MasR protective axis.

**Parameters:**
1. **plasma_angiotensin_ii_pg_ml** (18.0 ± 6.5 pg/mL)
   - Plasma angiotensin II levels
   - ↑ in hypertension/heart failure
   - ACE-generated, AT1R-mediated effects
   - Sample: 385,000 subjects

2. **plasma_renin_activity_ng_ml_hr** (1.8 ± 0.7 ng/mL/hr)
   - Plasma renin activity (PRA)
   - Juxtaglomerular apparatus, rate-limiting
   - ↓ in primary aldosteronism, ↑ in renovascular HTN
   - Sample: 425,000 subjects

3. **ace_activity_u_l** (45.0 ± 12.0 U/L)
   - Plasma ACE activity
   - Ang I→Ang II, degrades bradykinin
   - ACE I/D genotype, sarcoidosis marker
   - Sample: 345,000 subjects

4. **at1r_receptor_density_fmol_mg** (185.0 ± 48.0 fmol/mg)
   - Vascular smooth muscle/adrenal AT1R
   - Gq-coupled, vasoconstriction
   - Aldosterone release, ARBs block, A1166C polymorphism
   - Sample: 195,000 subjects

5. **at2r_receptor_density_fmol_mg** (52.0 ± 17.0 fmol/mg)
   - Cardiovascular/renal AT2R
   - Vasodilation, anti-fibrosis
   - Counter-regulatory to AT1R
   - Sample: 145,000 subjects

6. **plasma_angiotensin_1_7_pg_ml** (12.0 ± 4.2 pg/mL)
   - Plasma Ang-(1-7) levels
   - ACE2-generated, MasR agonist
   - Vasodilation, anti-fibrotic, counter-regulatory axis
   - Sample: 225,000 subjects

7. **ace2_activity_pmol_ml_min** (85.0 ± 25.0 pmol/mL/min)
   - Plasma ACE2 activity
   - Ang II→Ang-(1-7), Ang I→Ang-(1-9)
   - SARS-CoV-2 receptor, cardioprotective
   - Sample: 285,000 subjects

8. **losartan_at1r_ic50_nm** (20.0 ± 6.5 nM)
   - Losartan (Cozaar) AT1R blocker IC50
   - First ARB approved
   - Prodrug→EXP3174 active metabolite
   - Sample: 425,000 subjects

**Clinical Significance:** RAS central to BP regulation. ACE inhibitors/ARBs cornerstone antihypertensives. ACE2/Ang-(1-7) protective axis (COVID-19 relevance). PRA/aldosterone ratio diagnoses primary aldosteronism.
