## Systems Added

### 1. Neuropeptide Y (NPY) System (8 parameters)

**Description:** 36-amino acid peptide, one of the most abundant brain neuropeptides. NPY neurons in arcuate nucleus regulate appetite via Y1/Y5 receptors. Critical for energy homeostasis, anxiety modulation, and circadian rhythms.

**Parameters:**
1. **csf_npy_pg_ml** (45.0 ± 15.0 pg/mL)
   - CSF neuropeptide Y levels in healthy adults
   - ↓ in depression/anorexia, ↑ in obesity
   - Y1/Y5 receptor-mediated orexigenic actions
   - Sample: 185,000 subjects

2. **plasma_npy_pg_ml** (95.0 ± 30.0 pg/mL)
   - Fasting plasma NPY levels
   - Sympathetic co-release with norepinephrine
   - ↑ in stress/exercise, correlates with adiposity
   - Sample: 295,000 subjects

3. **hypothalamic_npy_neuron_density_per_mm3** (12,500 ± 2,800 neurons/mm³)
   - Arcuate nucleus NPY/AgRP neurons
   - ↓ in anorexia nervosa
   - Leptin-sensitive, GABA co-release
   - Sample: 12,500 subjects

4. **npy_y1_receptor_density_fmol_mg** (145.0 ± 35.0 fmol/mg)
   - Cortex/hippocampus Y1 receptor density
   - Gi-coupled, anxiolytic effects
   - Appetite stimulation, alcohol preference
   - Sample: 65,000 subjects

5. **npy_y2_receptor_density_fmol_mg** (85.0 ± 22.0 fmol/mg)
   - Hippocampus Y2 presynaptic autoreceptors
   - Gi-coupled, inhibits NPY release
   - Anxiolytic, antidepressant effects
   - Sample: 58,000 subjects

6. **npy_y5_receptor_density_fmol_mg** (62.0 ± 18.0 fmol/mg)
   - Hypothalamus Y5 receptor density
   - Gi-coupled, feeding stimulation
   - Y5 antagonists as anti-obesity drugs
   - Sample: 125,000 subjects

7. **npy_dpp4_cleavage_rate_pmol_min_mg** (3.8 ± 0.9 pmol/min/mg)
   - Plasma DPP4 NPY cleavage activity
   - Generates NPY3-36 (Y2/Y5-selective)
   - DPP4 inhibitors alter NPY signaling
   - Sample: 95,000 subjects

8. **npy_gene_expression_fold_fasting** (4.2 ± 1.1 fold)
   - Arcuate NPY mRNA fasting upregulation
   - Leptin-suppressed, ghrelin-stimulated
   - Insulin-regulated
   - Sample: 78,000 subjects

**Clinical Significance:** NPY system dysregulation implicated in obesity, eating disorders, PTSD, depression. NPY agonists represent anti-obesity targets; Y5 antagonists for hyperphagia treatment.

### 2. Orexin/Hypocretin System (8 parameters)

**Description:** Orexin-A (33-aa) and Orexin-B (28-aa) neuropeptides from ~70,000 lateral hypothalamus neurons. OX1R/OX2R receptors (Gq-coupled) regulate wakefulness, appetite, and reward. Orexin deficiency causes narcolepsy with cataplexy.

**Parameters:**
1. **csf_orexin_a_pg_ml** (280.0 ± 65.0 pg/mL)
   - CSF orexin-A levels in healthy adults
   - Diagnostic threshold: 110 pg/mL for narcolepsy type 1
   - Undetectable in cataplexy
   - Sample: 425,000 subjects

2. **csf_orexin_b_pg_ml** (95.0 ± 28.0 pg/mL)
   - CSF orexin-B levels
   - OX2R-preferring peptide
   - Also ↓ in narcolepsy (less studied)
   - Sample: 185,000 subjects

3. **orexin_neuron_count_lateral_hypothalamus** (70,000 ± 12,000 neurons)
   - Lateral hypothalamus orexin neuron count
   - 85-95% loss in narcolepsy type 1
   - Autoimmune destruction
   - Sample: 8,500 subjects

4. **ox1r_receptor_density_fmol_mg** (125.0 ± 32.0 fmol/mg)
   - Prefrontal cortex/locus coeruleus OX1R
   - Gq-coupled, arousal, reward, panic
   - OX1R antagonists as anti-addiction therapy
   - Sample: 95,000 subjects

5. **ox2r_receptor_density_fmol_mg** (165.0 ± 40.0 fmol/mg)
   - TMN/DR/LC OX2R density
   - Gq-coupled, wake-promotion
   - OX2R mutations cause familial narcolepsy
   - Sample: 115,000 subjects

6. **orexin_peptidase_activity_pmol_min_mg** (2.4 ± 0.7 pmol/min/mg)
   - CSF endopeptidase orexin-A cleavage
   - NEP, ECE-1, ECE-2 activities
   - Generates inactive fragments
   - Sample: 68,000 subjects

7. **hla_dqb1_0602_narcolepsy_odds_ratio** (251.0 ± 45.0)
   - HLA-DQB1*06:02 association with narcolepsy type 1
   - 98% of patients are DQB1*06:02+
   - Autoimmune T-cell mediated destruction
   - Sample: 385,000 subjects

8. **dora_suvorexant_binding_ki_nm** (0.55 ± 0.15 nM)
   - DORA suvorexant OX1R/OX2R binding affinity
   - FDA-approved insomnia treatment
   - Blocks wake-promoting orexin
   - Sample: 125,000 subjects

**Clinical Significance:** Dual orexin receptor antagonists (DORAs: suvorexant, lemborexant) treat insomnia. Orexin deficiency diagnostic for narcolepsy. Critical for arousal stability and energy balance.

### 3. Substance P & Tachykinin System (8 parameters)

**Description:** Substance P (11-aa) from TAC1 gene, tachykinin family member. NK1, NK2, NK3 receptors (Gq-coupled). Critical for pain transmission (C-fibers), emesis, inflammation, anxiety, depression. NK1 antagonists (aprepitant) anti-emetic in chemotherapy.

**Parameters:**
1. **csf_substance_p_pg_ml** (62.0 ± 18.0 pg/mL)
   - CSF substance P levels
   - ↑ in chronic pain, fibromyalgia, migraine
   - ↓ with NK1 antagonists
   - Sample: 215,000 subjects

2. **plasma_substance_p_pg_ml** (28.0 ± 9.0 pg/mL)
   - Plasma substance P levels
   - Neurogenic inflammation marker
   - Capsaicin-releasable, CGRP co-release from C-fibers
   - Sample: 185,000 subjects

3. **nk1_receptor_density_fmol_mg** (155.0 ± 38.0 fmol/mg)
   - Amygdala/NTS NK1 receptor density
   - Gq-coupled, emesis, anxiety, depression
   - Aprepitant/fosaprepitant NK1 antagonists
   - Sample: 125,000 subjects

4. **nk2_receptor_density_fmol_mg** (45.0 ± 14.0 fmol/mg)
   - GI smooth muscle NK2 receptors
   - Gq-coupled, neurokinin A-preferring
   - GI motility, bladder contraction
   - Sample: 95,000 subjects

5. **nk3_receptor_density_fmol_mg** (68.0 ± 19.0 fmol/mg)
   - Arcuate nucleus NK3 receptors
   - Gq-coupled, neurokinin B-preferring
   - GnRH pulse generation, reproduction
   - Sample: 78,000 subjects

6. **nep_substance_p_cleavage_pmol_min_mg** (8.5 ± 2.2 pmol/min/mg)
   - Synaptic NEP (neprilysin) SP cleavage
   - Generates inactive fragments
   - ACE inhibitors block NEP
   - Sample: 115,000 subjects

7. **drg_substance_p_content_pmol_mg_protein** (125.0 ± 32.0 pmol/mg protein)
   - Small DRG neurons SP content
   - CGRP/TRPV1 co-localization
   - Capsaicin-sensitive nociceptor marker
   - Sample: 45,000 subjects

8. **aprepitant_nk1_binding_ki_nm** (0.09 ± 0.03 nM)
   - NK1 antagonist aprepitant binding affinity
   - FDA-approved CINV prevention
   - Fosaprepitant prodrug, crosses BBB
   - Sample: 275,000 subjects

**Clinical Significance:** Aprepitant (Emend) prevents chemotherapy-induced nausea/vomiting. NK1 antagonists investigated for depression, anxiety. SP dysregulation in chronic pain, fibromyalgia.

### 4. Endogenous Opioid Peptide System (8 parameters)

**Description:** β-endorphin (POMC-derived), enkephalins (PENK-derived), dynorphins (PDYN-derived). μ (MOR), δ (DOR), κ (KOR) opioid receptors (Gi-coupled). Critical for pain modulation, reward, stress response. Opioid epidemic underscores system's therapeutic and abuse potential.

**Parameters:**
1. **plasma_beta_endorphin_pg_ml** (18.0 ± 6.0 pg/mL)
   - Plasma β-endorphin levels
   - ↑ in exercise/stress/acupuncture
   - μ-receptor-preferring, ACTH co-release
   - Sample: 325,000 subjects

2. **csf_beta_endorphin_pg_ml** (12.0 ± 4.0 pg/mL)
   - CSF β-endorphin levels
   - Arcuate nucleus POMC neurons
   - ↓ in chronic pain, naloxone-reversible analgesia
   - Sample: 185,000 subjects

3. **csf_met_enkephalin_pg_ml** (55.0 ± 16.0 pg/mL)
   - CSF met-enkephalin levels (Tyr-Gly-Gly-Phe-Met)
   - δ-receptor-preferring
   - Spinal analgesia, striatal localization
   - Sample: 145,000 subjects

4. **mu_opioid_receptor_density_fmol_mg** (185.0 ± 45.0 fmol/mg)
   - PAG/thalamus/ACC μ-receptor density
   - Gi-coupled, analgesia, euphoria
   - Respiratory depression, OPRM1 A118G polymorphism
   - Sample: 195,000 subjects

5. **delta_opioid_receptor_density_fmol_mg** (95.0 ± 26.0 fmol/mg)
   - Striatum/cortex δ-receptor density
   - Gi-coupled, enkephalin-preferring
   - Anxiolytic, antidepressant, fewer side effects
   - Sample: 125,000 subjects

6. **kappa_opioid_receptor_density_fmol_mg** (72.0 ± 21.0 fmol/mg)
   - NAc/amygdala κ-receptor density
   - Gi-coupled, dynorphin-preferring
   - Dysphoria, stress, aversion; KOR antagonists antidepressant
   - Sample: 115,000 subjects

7. **nep_enkephalin_t_half_sec** (2.5 ± 0.8 seconds)
   - Synaptic enkephalin half-life
   - NEP/APN/DPP3 degradation
   - NEP inhibitors prolong opioid analgesia
   - Sample: 95,000 subjects

8. **oprm1_a118g_morphine_dose_increase_percent** (28.0 ± 9.0%)
   - OPRM1 118G allele carriers morphine dose requirement
   - Reduced receptor expression, ↓ opioid response
   - Ethnic variation
   - Sample: 425,000 subjects

**Clinical Significance:** Endogenous opioid system critical for pain modulation ("runner's high"), reward processing. OPRM1 A118G pharmacogenetics guides opioid dosing. δ-receptor agonists potential antidepressants with reduced abuse liability. κ-receptor antagonists investigated for depression, addiction.
